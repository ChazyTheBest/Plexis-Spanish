1. Install World Of Warcraft
  2. Install The Burning Crusade (If you want to)
  3. Install The Wrath of the Lich King (If you want to)
  4. Patch your client to patch 3.3.5a
  5. Edit your "realmlist.wtf" (C:\Program Files\worldofwarcaft\data\enUS\realmlist.wtf) and change "Set Realmlist us.logon.worldofwarcraft.com" TO "set Realmlist {logon_server}"
  6. Create an Account
  7. Check your E-mail And Activate Account
  8. Support us with a Vote
  9. Follow the rules and Have Fun!
[NOTE] Do not patch over 3.3.5a, or you will not be able to log into out servers!
